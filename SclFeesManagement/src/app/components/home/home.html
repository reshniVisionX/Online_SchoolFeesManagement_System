About ELGI School

Established in 1985, ELGI School has been at the forefront of educational excellence for over three decades. Our institution is known for its holistic approach to education, combining academic rigor with character development.


We offer a comprehensive curriculum from kindergarten through high school, with specialized programs in science, arts, and technology. Our campus spans over 20 acres with modern classrooms, laboratories, sports facilities, and a well-stocked library.


At ELGI, we believe in fostering a love for learning, encouraging critical thinking, and developing responsible global citizens who will make positive contributions to society.

Our Vision

To be a premier educational institution that empowers students to achieve their fullest potential and become compassionate, innovative leaders who positively impact their communities and the world.


We envision a learning environment where every student discovers their unique talents, develops a lifelong love for learning, and acquires the skills necessary to thrive in an ever-changing global landscape.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing and stimulating educational environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth through innovative teaching methods and a comprehensive curriculum.


We are committed to cultivating critical thinking, creativity, and ethical values in our students while preparing them to meet the challenges of the future with confidence and resilience.
